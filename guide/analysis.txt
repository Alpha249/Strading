Operating Profit Margin = [Operating Income/Revenue] × 100

A balance sheet is a financial statement that reports a company's assets, liabilities and shareholders' equity.
Shareholders Equity = Total Assets − Total Liabilities

A cash flow statement is a financial statement that summarizes the amount of cash and cash equivalents entering and
leaving a company.

An income statement is one of the three (along with balance sheet and statement of cash flows) major financial statements
that reports a company's financial performance over a specific accounting period.
Net Income = (Total Revenue + Gains) – (Total Expenses + Losses)
